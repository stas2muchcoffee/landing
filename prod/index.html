About REWALA

Ask your friends about their opinion on questions that matter most to you.
Sometimes it’s hard to make a decision and you need an advice, that’s when Rewala can help you. Create polls, invite your family, friends or colleagues and get immediate voting results.
You have decision making skills and would like to share your views? Help your friends to make a good decision! Social decision making is what Rewala stand for.

Desktop version

Features

  • Add your questions and invite people to vote for them. Making decisions is now fast and super easy. Set the timer for your polls if you need an immediate opinion on any topic and just wait a bit to get the results.
    Before you buy an outmoded jeans or pullover, why not asking for an advice? Share several photos with your friends and let them help you.
  • Your friends or colleagues need an advice? You can do that just with a few taps. Help others to make the decision which can be minor or change their whole life.
    Vote for the best place for the weekend meetup, the movie to watch or country to visit. Let others hear your voice.
  • Keep your past questions and poll results in one place to get back to them easily. Check the history to be sure you made correct decisions. No need to download or install anything.
  • Get visually appealing and clear results on your questions and polls. A glance is enough for decision making. Saves your time and effort.
Voting and decision making anywhere!

How it work

  • Use your email to register in Rewala and get all the tools to make a decision that may change your life.
  • Receive invites to polls and vote for the best variants to help your friend make their decision. Your opinion should be heard!
  • Get a graphical representation of voting results. Clear and colorful charts for any decision you need help with.
  • Create groups of your friends or family members and share your question with a group. Ask only relevant people on what you are interested in.
